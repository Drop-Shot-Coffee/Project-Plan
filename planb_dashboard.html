§ 01 — Proof of Concept
A repeatable, software-enabled
brand at scale.
An expandable tournament model: three events, two-to-three carts, an adaptable per-season menu, and App V1. Scaling happens through events and ambassadors — not real estate.
POC Achieved
Mar 9, 2027 After full mini league season
Tournaments · T-1, T-2, T-3 EXPANDABLE MODEL
Three tournaments executed
Tournament 1 at Centerline (Sep 4–6) — 350-player baseline. Tournament 2 (Oct 9–11) at 500 players, App V1 deployed. Tournament 3 on Halloween weekend (Oct 31–Nov 2) — 500 players, theme-adapted menu. The scaling thesis: more players, more carts, more ambassadors — not more permanent locations.
TRACE → Plan B T-1, T-2, T-3 · 3 events · scaling proof point at T2/T3
Cart · E-2 MOBILE
Branded mobile cart in service
Custom branded cart built and tested ahead of Tournament 1, then redeployed across all three events — one cart, three tournaments. That is the whole argument: a permanent location serves one venue and sinks its capex; a mobile cart follows the demand. Fleet model — a second and third cart are provisioned post-POC, as event volume justifies them.
TRACE → Plan B 7.1.1–7.1.2 · Cart workstream
Software · S-1 UPGRADE
App V1 live and deployed
App MVP scoped (—), build initiated, QA and deployment (—). Core features: player coordination, event sign-up, points and rewards system. Deployed at Tournament 2 with full UX. Foundational infrastructure for the ambassador-led events engine.

Menu · M-2, M-3 ADAPTABLE MODEL
Adaptable menu per season / theme
