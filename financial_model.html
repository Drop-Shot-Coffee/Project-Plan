§ 02 — Founder Bonus Waterfall

Profit splits, tier by tier.

Editable Tiers
Progressive split per tournament's net profit · Company always majority
How this works: Each tournament applies its own independent waterfall. Each tier applies only to the portion of profit within its range, exactly like income tax brackets. T1 nets $X → flows through tiers → founder pool. Same for T2, T3 with their own profits. Total founder bonus = sum of all per-tournament pools. The pool divides equally among the founders listed in Founder Compensation.

§ 05 — Breakeven Analysis

Where the line crosses zero.

Two views: per-tournament (operational) and full scenario (investment)
Both recalculate as you flex any input above
Two breakeven views, two audiences. The tournament breakeven tells the operating team how many players each event needs to be worth running. The scenario breakeven tells Headway when the project's cumulative profit returns to zero — the moment Drop Shot Coffee stops being a cash investment and starts being a business.
Tournament breakeven — per event
How net profit responds to # players, all other inputs held constant. Vertical markers show waterfall tier triggers.
Scenario breakeven — cumulative net income
Different from cash flow: shows profit/loss accrual through the scenario. Crosses zero when cumulative revenue covers all costs to date.
