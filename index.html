§ 01 — The Thesis

The bottleneck has shifted.

The original prospectus assumed venue-gated scaling.
The new model rejects that constraint.
Original Model · Deferred

Venue-anchored coffee brand

Growth comes from signing more permanent locations. Each spot caps at ~450 cups/day. Capex is sunk per location. Scaling is bureaucratic, slow, and gated by landlords and operators.

  • Permanent installs as the scaling lever
  • Linear, location-by-location growth
  • Fixed menu, fixed footprint
  • Venue search = 6–12 month cycles
Active Model · Plan B

Mobile tournament beverage operator

Growth comes from more events × more ambassadors. Pickleball coaches making $80/hr in lessons are eager to run $2–5K events. Carts are mobile and redeploy across the calendar. Capex is variable, not sunk.

  • Events + ambassadors as the scaling lever
  • Combinatorial growth via the calendar
  • Menu adapts per season and theme
  • Doubling participants doubles revenue

§ 04 — What We're Building

Six deliverables, one engine.

Each deliverable feeds the others.
See the POC Plan for full WBS detail.
Tournaments · T-1 / T-2 / T-3
Three tournaments executed
T1 at Centerline (350p baseline), T2 with App V1 deployed (500p), T3 on Halloween weekend (500p, theme-adapted). The scaling proof point: doubling players doubles revenue without doubling capex.
Carts · E-2 / E-3
Two-to-three cart fleet
Custom branded cart for T1 (Jul 23 ready). Second and third carts provisioned to service the expandable model — mobile, redeployable across the event calendar.
Software · S-1
App V1 live and deployed
Build, QA, and deploy by Aug 12. Player coordination, event sign-up, points and rewards. Deployed at Tournament 2 with full UX — the foundation layer for the ambassador-led engine.
Menu · M-2 / M-3
Adaptable per season / theme
Core curated menu with roaster partner, plus seasonal and tournament-themed variations tested across T1, T2, and T3. Validates per-event menu adaptability.
Mini League · V-1.5 / V-1.6
9 months of recurring play
Test season (Jun–Aug) builds community baseline. Two full league seasons (Sep–Dec, Dec–Mar) at 60 / 75 participants. Recurring monetization alongside the event engine.
Ambassadors · A-0 / A-1 / A-2
Two ambassadors activated
Jonas Satuito as lead ambassador (signed). Second ambassador identified, signed, and activation plan commissioned by Jun 7. The scaling lever for the entire model.
